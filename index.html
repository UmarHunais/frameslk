Our Story

About Us

Our Philosophy

Born from Passion,
Driven by Art

frameslk was founded in 2016 by a passionate collective of visual artists united by one belief: that photography is the purest form of storytelling. What began as a solo venture in Colombo’s creative quarter has blossomed into Sri Lanka’s most recognised luxury photography studio.

We approach every commission as a collaboration — listening intently to each client’s vision and translating it into images of enduring beauty. From the grandest wedding ceremonies to the most intimate portrait sessions, we bring the same relentless pursuit of perfection to every frame.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01

Authenticity

We capture genuine emotions — not posed performances. Every photograph reflects the true essence of the moment.

02

Craftsmanship

From the moment we press the shutter to the final edited image, every detail is handled with precision and care.

03

Timelessness

Our aesthetic transcends trends. We create images that will feel as powerful in decades as they do today.

Meet the Artist

Kasun Perera

With over eight years of experience and more than 500 weddings photographed across South Asia, Kasun Perera is the creative force behind frameslk. A graduate of the Academy of Visual Arts, Colombo, his work has been featured in Vogue India, Harper’s Bazaar Bride, and numerous international photography journals.

Kasun’s signature style — soft natural light, editorial composition, and emotionally resonant storytelling — has earned him a devoted following among discerning couples and brands across Sri Lanka and beyond.
